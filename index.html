Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access all the lessons, exercises, and quizzes without any cost.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You can start using the website without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to track your progress, save your favorite lessons, and access additional features.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Read, listen, and watch English daily, noting new words with meanings and examples, and practice using them in speaking, writing, and games to remember them long-term.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes, we offer certificates for completed courses. You can download your certificate from your account page after finishing a course.
